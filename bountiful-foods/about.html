Our History

Bountiful Foods began in 2012 when we discovered a way to provide healthy food with a satisfying taste.

Over the years we grew from just a small local business physically located in Carlsbad, to having many locations throughout the Southern California coast from Los Angeles to Sand Diego.

Whether you need fresh ingredients for home-cooking, or a healthy snack to go, we're proud to offer the quality products you need.
