Our Audience

Business owners, entrepreneurs, active members of the community. Our audience includes anyone looking to get connected to the community through events or a means of business. Target audience age ranges from 18-70. Audience primarily uses phones and laptops when using the internet.

Personas

Mark

Mark is 34 Years old and married with two kids. Mark has just opened his own chiropractic practice in the Maury county area. Mark is looking for business opportunities and interested in joining the Chamber of Commerce Maury Alliance. Mark is an avid internet user and mostly uses his cell phone to browse the internet.

Anna

Anna is 26 years old with a newborn on the way. Anna and her husband are new to the Maury County area and want to be more part of the community. Anna likes to do freelance web design work and is looking for business owners to create websites for. Anna sees the Chamber of Commerce Maury Alliance as the perfect start. Anna mostly uses her laptop to browse the internet.

Scenarios

  • I am looking for upcoming community events, where can I find this information?

  • I am looking to get some opinions on marketing my business from other business owners.

  • I am planning on moving to Maury County soon. How is the community there? Will my business thrive?

  • I want to open up my own restaurant. Are there any chamber members involved with the grocery market that I can make a deal with for fresh ingrediants?

  • I am looking for expert opinions to get an idea of the price to startup my business.
